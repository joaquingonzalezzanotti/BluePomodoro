# **App Name**: FocusFlow

## Core Features:

- Task Breakdown: Break down large tasks into smaller, manageable sub-tasks.
- AI-Powered Prioritization Tool: Suggest task priorities based on deadlines, estimated effort, and personal energy levels, using a reasoning LLM as a tool.
- Pomodoro Timer: Integrate a Pomodoro timer to encourage focused work sessions with built-in break times.
- Focus Music Integration: Connect to music streaming services or local playlists for focus-enhancing background music.
- Gamified Progress Tracking: Visually track progress and reward task completion with points, badges, or leaderboards.
- External Distraction Blocker: Temporary blocking of access to external urls to maintain focus
- Google Calendar and Task Sync: Synchronize tasks and deadlines with Google Calendar and Google Tasks.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light, calming blue (#79BAEC) to promote focus and reduce anxiety.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated blue (#E8F4FA) to maintain a light and airy feel.
- Accent color: Warm yellow (#FFD700) for highlighting important tasks and deadlines.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif for a balance of modern style and legibility.
- Clean, minimalist icons for task categories, reminders, and rewards.
- Clear, spacious layout with prioritized elements and minimal clutter to reduce visual distractions.
- Subtle, encouraging animations to provide positive reinforcement for task completion.
